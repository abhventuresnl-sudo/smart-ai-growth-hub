Top Picks: Deep Dives

1. Tidio AI, Best for AI Customer Support

Tidio is an AI-powered live chat and chatbot platform built with ecommerce in mind. It connects directly to your Shopify store and can automatically answer customer questions, track orders, and recover abandoned carts.

What we like:

  • Easy to set up
  • Works right out of the box
  • Free plan available

What to watch out for:

  • Limited customisation on free tier
  • Can feel repetitive for complex queries

Who it’s best for: Beginners who want to automate customer support without hiring staff

2. Jasper AI, Best for AI Copywriting

Jasper is one of the most popular AI writing tools available. For Shopify store owners, it excels at generating product descriptions, email campaigns, and ad copy in minutes rather than hours.

What we like:

  • Huge template library
  • Shopify product description mode
  • Beginner-friendly

What to watch out for:

  • No free plan
  • Can produce generic output without good prompts

Who it’s best for: Store owners who struggle with writing and want to publish content consistently

3. Klaviyo AI, Best for AI Email Marketing

Klaviyo is the email marketing platform of choice for serious Shopify sellers. Its AI features help you write better subject lines, predict when customers will buy again, and segment your audience automatically.

What we like:

  • Deep Shopify integration
  • AI subject line suggestions
  • Predictive analytics

What to watch out for:

  • Pricing scales fast as your list grows
  • Learning curve for advanced flows

Who it’s best for: Store owners ready to build an email list and automate follow-ups

4. Replo, Best for AI Landing Page Builder

Replo lets you build high-converting landing pages and sales pages directly inside Shopify using drag-and-drop, no developer needed. The AI features help generate section copy and layout ideas.

What we like:

  • Purpose-built for Shopify
  • No coding needed
  • AI section generation

What to watch out for:

  • Higher price point
  • Overkill for very small stores

Who it’s best for: Store owners running paid ads who need fast, professional landing pages

5. Ecomtent, Best for AI Product Images & Content

Ecomtent uses AI to generate lifestyle product images and SEO-optimised content at scale. Instead of expensive photoshoots, you can create scroll-stopping visuals for your listings.

What we like:

  • Generates lifestyle product images with AI
  • Bulk content creation
  • SEO descriptions included

What to watch out for:

  • Newer tool, still evolving
  • Image quality varies by product type

Who it’s best for: Dropshippers or store owners who need great product images without a photographer

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need technical skills to use AI tools with Shopify?

No. The tools in this list are specifically chosen because they are beginner-friendly. Most connect to Shopify with a simple install from the Shopify App Store, with no coding required.

Are AI tools worth the cost for a new Shopify store?

It depends on the tool. We recommend starting with free plans or trials before committing. Many tools, like Klaviyo and Tidio, have genuinely useful free tiers that are plenty for a store just starting out.

Can AI replace a professional copywriter for my store?

AI tools are excellent for producing first drafts quickly, but human review is still valuable, especially for brand voice, accuracy, and nuance. Think of AI as a very fast writing assistant, not a full replacement.

Which AI tool should I start with as a complete beginner?

We recommend starting with Tidio (customer support) and Klaviyo (email) because both have strong free plans, connect directly to Shopify, and solve real pain points from day one.

Will these AI tools work outside of Shopify?

Most of the tools listed, like Jasper and ChatGPT, work with any ecommerce platform. Klaviyo and Tidio also support WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and others. Replo is Shopify-specific.

How quickly will I see results from using AI tools?

You can see results almost immediately for tasks like writing product descriptions or setting up a chatbot. For email marketing and SEO, results build over weeks and months as you grow your audience.
